Unsettled Questions

ReincarnatedAI should be brave enough to investigate, and disciplined enough to leave blanks blank.

When exactly do NDE perceptions occur?

During arrest, early recovery, or retrospective reconstruction? Stronger time-locking is needed.

What would count as repeated veridical target success?

Hidden-target studies need scale, consistency, and clear preregistration.

Can reincarnation cases be collected prospectively?

Documenting claims before any identification is the cleanest path forward.

Are mystical states contact or construction?

Psychedelic research proves meaningful experience, not yet metaphysical access.

What mechanism could preserve personal identity?

Survival hypotheses need models that explain memory, continuity, and selectivity.

Which evidence would falsify favored theories?

A serious research program must name what would weaken its own preferred interpretations.
