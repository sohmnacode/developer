Quantum Biology

Quantum Mind Theory

Quantum coherence in biological systems — once dismissed — is now observed in photosynthesis and bird navigation. Penrose and Hameroff propose that microtubules in neurons may be the site of quantum-level consciousness processes.

Roger Penrose · The Emperor's New Mind
Stuart Hameroff · Orch OR, Arizona
Gregory Engel · Quantum coherence, 2007

About

Why ReincarnatedAI Exists

Science has mapped the genome, split the atom, and probed the edges of the universe — but the nature of consciousness, the reality of subjective experience, and what happens when we die remain almost entirely unexplored. ReincarnatedAI exists to change that. We built an AI that takes the question of the soul seriously — not as religion, not as wishful thinking, but as the most important open problem in science.

Scientific Rigor

Every claim grounded in peer-reviewed research. We distinguish what the data shows, what it suggests, and what remains speculative.

Compassionate Inquiry

Many who come here have had experiences that changed them. We meet those experiences with genuine curiosity and respect, not dismissal.

Open Frontier

We don't claim answers. We help you ask better questions — and connect you to the research that's quietly rewriting the map.

ReincarnatedAI is not a spiritual guide, therapist, or authority on metaphysical truth.
It is a research assistant — for the greatest mystery.
