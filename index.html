Emerging Frontier

Technology & Soul Science

Mind uploading, AI consciousness, and neurotechnology are making the oldest questions precise. Four areas where the science is moving fast.

Whole Brain Emulation

Mind Uploading

OpenWorm ran a complete connectome in software. Blue Brain simulated a cortical column at cellular resolution. Substrate-independent consciousness is an engineering problem, not a theoretical one.

Integrated Information Theory

AI & Consciousness

Tononi's IIT gives consciousness a mathematical measure applicable to any system. Current AI scores low on phi despite sophisticated outputs — raising harder questions about what biological brains actually do.

Neurotechnology

The Dying Brain

A 2023 PNAS study found a gamma wave surge at death exceeding waking activity. Sam Parnia's AWARE II places hidden targets in resuscitation rooms. The moment of death is now measurable at millisecond resolution.

Philosophy of Identity

Continuity & The Self

If mind uploading works, identity is pattern — not biology. Parfit's psychological continuity theory predicts exactly the kind of cross-life memory chains documented in reincarnation research.
