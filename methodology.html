Research Standards

Methodology

How ReincarnatedAI separates experience, evidence, interpretation, and speculation.

Evaluation Protocol

Every claim is treated as a layered object. First: what was reported? Second: what can be verified? Third: what ordinary explanations remain? Fourth: what larger theory, if any, is justified?

1

Document early

Give greatest weight to statements recorded before confirmation, media exposure, family contact, or researcher interpretation.

2

Separate facts from meaning

A verified perception is evidence for anomalous perception; it is not automatically evidence for a full metaphysical system.

3

Prefer prospective designs

Studies that define targets, samples, and methods before outcomes are stronger than retrospective collections.

4

Track normal information paths

Check overhearing, coaching, cryptomnesia, prior family contact, internet availability, and investigator leakage.

What would change our mind?

Stronger evidence would include repeated prospective veridical perception, preregistered psi replications with large effects, and reincarnation cases documented before identification with independent records. Weaker evidence would include failed high-powered replications, clear information leakage, or physiological models that predict the disputed features.
